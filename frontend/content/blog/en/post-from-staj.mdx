## Real Project Experience

Working on a production codebase teaches you things no bootcamp can. Here are the most valuable lessons.

### Code Reviews

Every pull request goes through at least one review. This caught bugs early and taught me to write cleaner, more maintainable code.

### Production Debugging

When something breaks in production, there's no "refresh and try again." You learn to read logs, reproduce issues, and deploy hotfixes under pressure.

### Agile Workflows

Daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives — these ceremonies keep the team aligned and the project moving forward.
